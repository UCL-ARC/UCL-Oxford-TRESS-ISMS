## Formal documents
These are the auditable documents of the ISMS. They comprise four levels:

Level 1. **Policies**, defining the organisation’s position and requirements. These are approved by the Operational Management Group (OMG), Technical Working Group (TWG), and/or Institutional Risk Governance Committee (IRGC).

Level 2. **Procedures**, for enacting the policies’ requirements at a high level. These are noted by the OMG and/or TWG.

Level 3. **Work instructions**, setting out how we implement individual elements of the procedures. For example, a _procedure_ may refer to a specific piece of equipment or software to use, which a _work instruction_ can explain in more detail how to operate. These are subject to operational change, but must comply with the two levels above.	

Level 4. **Records**, tracking the procedures and work instructions, providing evidence that they have been followed correctly and consistently.
