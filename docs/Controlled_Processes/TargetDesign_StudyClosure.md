# Overview
This process allows an Information Asset Owner to close a Study they own but no longer wish to use. This process is owned by the Research Information Governance (IG) Lead.

## 5 Safes Link
**Safe Projects**: research projects are approved by data owners for the public good. In this instance, the entire lifecyle of research projects is managed using controlled processes, to ensure research projects (Studies) are closed once the research is complete.

# Process
## Purpose
This process ensures that only empty Studies are closed, by checking that there are no active Projects in any of the TRE's or technical envrionments available to the Study.

## Scope
All Studies registered with the ARC Information Governance Advisory Service are within the scope of the ISMS and this process.
