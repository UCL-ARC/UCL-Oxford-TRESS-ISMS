# Overview

This process creates a Study within the TRE. A Study is a container for researchers, data and one or more Project spaces in a TRE Technical Environment. This process is owned by the TRE Environment Owner. This process is owned by the Research Information Governance (IG) Lead.

## 5 Safes Link

**Safe projects**: research projects are approved by data owners for the public good.

# Process
## Purpose

Process ensures that all data within the TRE has an owner, UCL has a legal right to process the data, adheres to any contractual responsibilities and data owners understand their responsibilities.

## Scope

All Studies registered with the ARC Information Governance Advisory Service are within the scope of the ISMS and this process.

## Responsibilities

- Information Governance Team: has oversight of the research data provided. Once satisfied, creates Study and assigns Information Asset Owner role within the Service Management Environment.
- Applicant Information Asset Owner: provides Study details and agrees to responsibilities, understands and owns risks related to the data and completes any other necessary compliance tasks (provide research contracts, complete Information Asset Register, gain ethics approval etc.).

## Prerequisites

- Applicant Information Asset Owner must be an Approved Researcher.

## Steps

1. Applicant IAO submits Study details.
2. Applicant IAO completes compliance tasks (sign responsibilities, obtain Approved Researcher role, provide research contracts, complete Information Asset Register).
3. Information Governance Team checks the compliance tasks, confirming the research data is suitable for the TRE.
4. Information Governance Team creates the Study.
5. Information Governance Team assigns IAO role to applicant within the Service Management Environment.
6. IAO assigns any Information Asset Administrators they would like to manage the Study.
