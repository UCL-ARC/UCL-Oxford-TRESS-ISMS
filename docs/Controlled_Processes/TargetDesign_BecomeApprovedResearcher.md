# Overview

This process moves a user without the "Approved Research" role to a user with the "Approved Researcher" role (see the [Roles Catalogue](/docs/Controlled_Documents/RISM02-Roles_and_Responsibilities.md)). This process is owned by the ARC TRE Envrionment Owner.

## 5 Safes Link

**Safe people**: researchers are trained and authorised to use data safely.

# Process
## Purpose

This process outlines the process to manage user access to the TRE and projects within the TRE. This process ensures only "safe people" have access to the environment.

## Scope

All users of a UCL TRE, from the point of their initial application to becoming an Approved Researcher, are within the scope of the ISMS and this process.

## Responsibilities

- Applicant Approved Researcher: verify their identity, complete competency training (or otherwise prove competency), make attestations (such as agreeing to T&Cs/Acceptable Use Statement).
- Information Governance Team: process applications, verify that the information provided by the user is correct, and assign "Approved Resarcher" role to users within the Service Management Environment.

## Prerequisites

- Applicant Approved Researcher may require a UCL account. Where required they must complete the guest sign-up process.

## Steps

1. Applicant Approved Researcher completes Approved Researcher requirements (competency training and attestations).
2. Information Governance Team checks requirements are complete.
3. Information Governance Team assigns "Approved Researcher" role to the applicant.
