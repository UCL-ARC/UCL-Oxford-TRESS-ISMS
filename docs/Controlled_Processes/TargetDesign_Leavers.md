# Overview
This process allows an Approved Researcher's existing access to Projects to be relinquished or revoked upon employment termination or role change. This process is owned by the Research Information Governance (IG) Lead.

## 5 Safes Link
**Safe people:** researchers are trained and authorised to use data safely, in this case Information Asset Owners are required to remove members from their research projects upon employment termination or role change.

# Process
## Purpose
Process ensures that Approved Researchers whose employment ends or role changes have their access to TRE(s), and the data within them, revoked promptly through a controlled process. The Study Agreement assigns the responsibility of access revokation in accordance with the Access Control Policy to the Information Asset Owner.

## Scope

All Projects and constituent Approved Researchers within UCL's TRE's are within the scope of the ISMS and this process.

## Responsibilities
- Information Asset Owner: responsible for ensuring that access is revoked in accordance with the Access Control Policy, when a Project member's employment ends or role changes.
- Leaver (Approved Researcher): notify Information Asset Owner that they are leaving their employment/role and
- TRE Environment Team: responds promptly to Information Asset Owner's request to revoke access, and implements the change in the technical environment.

## Prerequisites

- Access revokation for a leaver can only be initiated by an Information Asset Owner.

## Steps
1. The Information Asset Owner uses the Change Project Roles Group controlled process to revoke the leaver's membership of the Project upon learning of the leaver's departure.
2. The TRE Envrironment Team promptly responds to the  Project Roles Group change request, following the Change Project Roles Group controlled proceess, updating and deploying the Project configuration to revoke the leaver's access to the technical Environemnt.
