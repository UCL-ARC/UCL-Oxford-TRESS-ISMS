## 7. Approved Researcher, Study and Project Roles

#### 7.1 Approved Researcher

Approved researchers:
- Have no default data access  
- Must complete mandatory training  
- Must sign an <APPROVED_RESEARCHER_AGREEMENT_NAME>  
- Must be covered by a legal contract or formal agreement  


#### 7.2 Study Roles

Each study must have:
- An **Information Asset Owner**  
- Optionally, one or more **Information Asset Administrators**

| Role | Key Responsibility |
|------|--------------------|
| Information Asset Owner | Owns and is accountable for study data |
| Information Asset Administrator | Manages users and projects on behalf of the Owner |

Both roles must be:
- Approved researchers  
- Substantive members of <ORG_NAME>  


#### 7.3 Project Roles

Projects act as technical and permissions boundaries.

Users may hold roles such as:
- Project Writer  
- Project Read-Only User  
- Project Egress User  
The Information Asset Owner or Administrator must approve all access.
