## 1. Introduction

### 1.1 Purpose

This guide defines how `<University Name>` establishes, implements, maintains, and continually improves its Information Security Management System (ISMS). The ISMS is aligned with ISO/IEC 27001 and is designed to protect the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information across teaching, research, and professional services.

### 1.2 Objectives

-   Protect institutional, research, staff, student, and partner information
-   Manage information security risks systematically
-   Support compliance with legal, regulatory, contractual, and ethical obligations
-   Enable assurance, auditability, and continual improvement
-   Provide a foundation for ISO/IEC 27001 certification (where applicable) 

### 1.3 Audience

This document applies to: - All staff, students, contractors, and third parties - All information systems and services within the ISMS scope
