## 3.	Organisational Context
The <ISMS_NAME> exists to protect research data used within <ORG_NAME> and by its collaborators.

<ORG_NAME> is a research-intensive organisation conducting research across multiple data domains such as health, social sciences, and technology. It collaborates with stakeholders across academia, government, industry, and the third sector. These collaborations often involve handling sensitive, highly confidential research data.

The organisation is committed to:
- Protecting data subjects.  
- Meeting legal, regulatory, and contractual obligations.  
- Supporting ethical, responsible, and trustworthy research.  

The ISMS addresses the following key challenges:

- Researchers and collaborators handle large volumes of sensitive research data.  
- A wide variety of data classifications, including personal, sensitive, and commercially confidential data.  
- Rapid growth in data volumes and computational complexity.  
- Existing baseline information security controls are insufficient for specialised research environments.

## 8.Out of Scope
The following elements are explicitly outside the ISMS scope:

- Data exported from TRE boundaries as defined in <DATA_CLASSIFICATION_POLICY_NAME>.  
- Endpoint devices used to access TREs.  
- Technical environments below Tier 3 classification.  
- Systems not directly supporting secure research data processing.
